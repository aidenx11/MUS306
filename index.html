WinterBlue

Aiden Schroeder — MUS 306, 2026

Program Notes

WinterBlue is an ambient piece inspired by the works of Brian Eno and C418.

The melody was generated using the Koenig algorithm to produce a sequence of pitches. Breaking the melody halfway through the piece, a texture of clicks and rain-like sounds was created using a Markov chain to determine timing and density, with the resulting material rendered through granular synthesis.

The pad and bass sounds are built-in instrument presets that were processed through waveshapers. Nearly all elements in the piece have some form of reverb applied, which was used to create a sense of shared space.

A wind-like texture runs throughout the piece and grows louder toward the end. This sound was made from a recording of a garbage truck outside, which was then filtered and manipulated.

Bio

Aiden Schroeder is a senior in computer science, and has never composed music before.
